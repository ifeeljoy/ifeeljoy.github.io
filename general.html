General Commands

The General category includes essential commands that enhance user interaction and provide useful information.

Commands

  • /userinfo: Displays information about a user, including their account details and status.
  • /avatar: Shows the avatar of a specified user.
  • /8ball: Ask the magic 8-ball a question for a fun, random response.
  • /serverinfo: Provides details about your server, such as member count and server settings.

These commands add a fun and informative touch to your server, making interactions more engaging and helpful for your community.
